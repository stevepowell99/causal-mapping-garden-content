## Summary{.banner}

This QuIP study, conducted by [Bath SDR](https://bathsdr.org/), looked at the impact of Fairtrade co-operatives on Cocoa farmers in Cote D'Ivoire. The data was collected through a partnership with Fairtrade and On Our Radar. 42 participants were engaged in conversations regarding various domains such as agriculture and environmental issues via text messages over 4 months. The data was analysed and presented using the Causal Map app.

[See the full report here](https://bathsdr.org/quip-and-fairvoice/)
